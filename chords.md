### Triads

major: 1 3 5

minor m: 1 3b 5

diminished o: 1 3b 5b

augmented +: 1 3 5# 


### 7th 

major △: 1 3 5 7 

dominant 7: 1 3 5 7b

minor m7: 1 3b 5 7b

half diminished ø7: 1 3b 5b 7b

(fully) diminished o7: 1 3b 5b 7bb
